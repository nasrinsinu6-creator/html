Know About Rose

The rose is a world-renowned flowering shrub celebrated for its beauty, captivating fragrance, and symbolic meanings, particularly love and beauty. Belonging to the Rosaceae family, there are over 150 wild species and thousands of cultivated varieties that grow in a wide range of colors, from deep red to white, and forms like shrubs, climbers, and miniatures.

A rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars. They form a group of plants that can be erect shrubs, climbing, or trailing, with stems that are often armed with sharp prickles.
